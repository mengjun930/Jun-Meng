About Me

I am a Research Associate at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, specializing in materials science with a focus on clean energy applications. My research interests include ion transport in solid-state systems, interfaces, and metastable phases, leveraging advanced atomic-scale modeling and machine learning methods.

Research

Interstitial Oxygen Diffusers Discovery

Developed a method for discovering new interstitial oxygen diffusers, identifying multiple new families and validating La4Mn5Si4O22 as a superior oxygen conductor. This research has led to two first-author papers and a patent.

StructOpt Algorithm Development

Developed the StructOpt algorithm, integrating experimental data with real-time atomic simulations to optimize atomic structure. This work resulted in one first-author paper, five contributed papers, and a patent.
